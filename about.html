Nossa História

The Generics is a rock band formed in 2015 that quickly gained fans worldwide with their unique sound and energetic performances.

It all started when four childhood friends, passionate about music, decided to form a band in their hometown. What began as a hobby quickly turned into a promising career when their first single went viral on streaming platforms.

Since then, the band has released four critically acclaimed albums and toured in more than 30 countries. Their musical style combines elements of classic rock with modern influences, creating a sound that is both familiar and innovative.

The band is known for their energetic live performances and the genuine connection they establish with their fans. Each show is a unique experience, with customized setlists and moments of improvisation that make each presentation special.

Beyond music, The Generics is dedicated to social and environmental causes, using their platform to promote positive changes in the world.
